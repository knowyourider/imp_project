About this slideshow

During the 1830s Orra White Hitchcock accompanied her husband Edward Hitchcock on geological excursions throughout the Connecticut Valley. He was working on his Geologic Survey for the state of Massachusetts while she drew a series of landscape views that were published as a supplement to his 1841 report to the state.
