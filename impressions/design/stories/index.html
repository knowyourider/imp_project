Stories

These stories tell about the discovery of the dinosaur footprints and about the lives of the discoverers.

  • Noah's RavenInterpreting the "mysterious marks"

    In which interpretations of the "fossil bird tracks" evolve from a biblical understanding to a scientific one.
  • A Common Man's Uncommon DiscoveryDinosaur footprints in sidewalk stones

    In which a poor laborer’s life changes dramatically when he notices what look like turkey tracks in a rock he was laying in a sidewalk
  • Whose Discovery Was It?The tracks in a scientific sense

    In which Edward Hitchcock and James Deane spar over who deserves credit for discovering the fossil tracks. Benjamin Silliman officiates.
  • Refinement and RomanceEdward and Orra's courtship

    In which Edward Hitchcock, young scientist, meets Orra White, budding naturalist.
  • Spreading the Gospel of GeologyThe Hitchcocks tour Europe

    In which the discovery of the dinosaur tracks spreads to Europe and beyond.

  • Working TogetherCompanions in science, art and religion

    In which Edward and Orra Hitchcock collaborate and together invent ichnology.
