Impressions From a Lost World

The discovery of dinosaur tracks in New England that changed 19th century America.

The Connecticut River Valley as it might have looked 250,000,000 years ago when dinosaurs roamed the area.

Dinosaurs, walking in the Jurassic mud, left behind tracks...

...that over time hardened into fossils and then were covered with millions of years worth of sediment.

In 1835, while laying sidewalk stones in Greenfield, Massachusetts, Dexter Marsh uncovered the curious marks.

Edward Hitchcock and his wife Orra, studied these marks, publishing articles and giving lectures.

Orra's drawings helped spread the knowledge and changed the accepted understanding about the earth's deep past.
