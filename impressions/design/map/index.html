You Are Here

Our story takes place in western Massachusetts, in the valley around the Connecticut River; between the Vermont/Massachusetts border to the north and the Connecticut coastline to the south.

How to Use the Map

The interactive map feature lets you find places to visit, historic sites and exhibits of fossils throughout the Connecticut River Valley. Also be sure to investigate the linked resources to the right.
